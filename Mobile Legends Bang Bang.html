Mobile Legends Bang Bang

Mobile Legends Bang Bang is a mobile multiplayer online battle arena game developed and published by Moonton, a subsidiary of ByteDance. Released in 2016, the game grew in popularity; most prominently in Southeast Asia.

Top 5 Facts About Mobile Legends Bang Bang!

  • Indonesia currently holds the record for the most players.

What is Mobile Legends Bang Bang?

Mobile Legends is an online fighting game where players with match up with other players and fight. The goal is to destroy the enemy’s tower, and while in game, you can kill other players, kill minions, kill monsters and more. Earn gold during the game to buy more items that will improve your hero’s performance. There are a lot of different heroes you can choose from, with mastery levels and backstories so you’ll never get bored.
