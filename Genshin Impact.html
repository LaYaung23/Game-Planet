Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game developed by miHoYo, published by miHoYo in mainland China and by HoYoverse worldwide. It was released for Android, iOS, PlayStation 4, and Windows in 2020, on PlayStation 5 in 2021, and is set for release on Nintendo Switch.

Top 5 Facts About Genshin Impact!

  • Controversy over copying The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild.
  • Genshin Impact cost $100 million USD to develop, making it one of the most expensive video games to develop.
  • The home screen time and music changes based on your device’s time.
  • You are able to fish in-game using Cryo characters.
  • Receive a special gift on your Birthday.

What is Genshin Impact?

In 2020, Genshin Impact took the video game industry by storm, attracting a large player base and generating nearly $400 million in revenue within its first two months on the market. For context, this was more than Pokémon Go, which generated $238 million in revenue within the same time period.
