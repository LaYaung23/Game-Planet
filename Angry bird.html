Angry Bird

Angry Birds is a 2009 casual puzzle video game developed by Finnish video game developer Rovio Entertainment. Inspired primarily by a sketch of stylized wingless birds, the game was first released for iOS and Maemo devices starting in December 2009.

Top 5 Facts About Angry Bird!

  • Angry Birds Star Wars II. Improves on the original with more characters and just more to do in general
  • Angry Birds was the first mobile game to hit one billion downloads.
  • Angry Birds was the first mobile game to be played in space at the International Space Station.
  • Angry Birds made by a team of four people and was completed of gight months.
  • The total cost of making the game was less than $70.000.

What is Angry Birds?

Angry Birds is a Finnish action, puzzle, and strategy based media franchise created by Rovio Entertainment. The game series focuses on the eponymous flock of colorful angry birds who try to save their eggs from green-colored pigs.
